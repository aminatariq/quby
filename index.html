Introducing Quby

Quby is an on-screen assistant that offers meeting organisers a new solution to efficiently manage questions from a live audience.

We believe in using technology to make life smarter... and Quby does just that.

How it Works


Present your talk with ease...

Whether you're presenting in a classroom, a boardroom or a public hall, install Quby on your desktop and let it be your digital moderator.

...while Quby takes questions...

Your listeners can go online and send in their questions using the unique URL for your presentation session and Quby will add them to the queue.

...that you can answer quickly and clearly

Go through the queue and address your listeners' questions more efficiently, saving everyone's time.

About Us

We are a team of four developers and marketers, who came together to solve the same problem we all have faced as participants of thousands of presentations:


How can we make sure the presenter can hear us loud and clear when we're part of a large audience?


Tired of not being seen or heard when we've raised our hands, and unsatisfied with the ineffictiveness of current solutions, we came up with Quby: a simple and convenient digital moderator for large audiences.


Some background about our team: we're two summer graduates from Stanford University, another graduate from West Chester University, and a soon-to-be grad of Waterloo University.


A notable Stanford professor of entrepreneurship also sits on our advisory board.
